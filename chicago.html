About Chicago Pneumatic

Founded in 1901, Chicago Pneumatic has grown to become a global leader in air compressors, pneumatic tools, hydraulic equipment, and power generators. With a focus on performance, reliability, and innovation, CP serves a wide range of industries including manufacturing, construction, automotive, and mining. Their products are designed to deliver maximum productivity and efficiency in the toughest conditions.
