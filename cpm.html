About Chicago Pneumatic

Chicago Pneumatic is a global manufacturer of high-performance tools, air compressors and equipment for construction, manufacturing and vehicle service industries. For over 120 years, Chicago Pneumatic has been designing and manufacturing high-quality, innovative products that meet the demands of professionals worldwide.

As an authorized dealer, Airline Engineers provides sales, installation, maintenance, and repair services for the complete range of Chicago Pneumatic products, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of your equipment.
